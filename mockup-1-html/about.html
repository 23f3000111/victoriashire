Our story

Fifty years, built around the early years.

Victoriashire International Education Group is a preschool, a kindergarten, a music school and an enrichment institute — founded on one conviction: the early years are where confidence, independence and a love of learning are really made.

One group, four ways in

An education group devoted to childhood.

For half a century we've focused on the years most schools treat as waiting-room time before "real school". Around eighty per cent of the brain develops before a child turns three — so we give those years the seriousness they deserve.

A note from our founder

I started Victoriashire because I believe the early years are the whole story. Our classrooms borrow from Montessori, from inquiry-based learning, and from fifty years of curriculum craft — but the goal is simple: children who ask good questions, care about other people, and walk into primary school already loving the work. Come spend a morning with us. The children will show you what I mean better than I can.

— Founder, Victoriashire International Education
