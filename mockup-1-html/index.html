Our approach

A foundation for lifelong success.

We don't rush childhood. In a prepared, unhurried classroom, children build the concentration, independence and confidence that make everything after school easier — from their first words to their first exam.

Why families choose Victoriashire

A calm start that sets children up to thrive.

A prepared Montessori environment

Low shelves, real materials, and the freedom to choose meaningful work — so children learn to concentrate and follow a task through to the end.

Three languages, every day

English leads the day, with Mandarin and Bahasa Melayu woven through — so a second and third language feel ordinary, not difficult.

A music school down the hall

Piano, violin, voice and more, taught by specialists — every child can learn to play an instrument, not just hear about one.

The Montessori difference

Built for independence, not compliance.

Our classrooms aren't arranged around the teacher. Children choose their work, correct their own mistakes, and move at their own pace — so they build the kind of confidence that lasts, not just the ability to sit still.

  • Self-directed work in uninterrupted blocks
  • Mixed-age classrooms where children learn from one another
  • Careful observation instead of constant testing
