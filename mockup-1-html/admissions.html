How to apply

Three steps, no mystery.

Application, assessment, registration — here's exactly what to bring and what happens at each stage.

  1. 1

    Application

    Submit the application form with the application fee and these documents:

    • Two passport-size photos of your child; one of each parent
    • Birth certificate and IC/passport (student visa for foreign students)
    • Each parent's IC/passport (and visa)
    • Vaccination record and your child's medical/health record
    • A copy of your insurance policy
  2. 2

    Assessment

    Our admissions team books a time to meet your child. Placement considers age, ability and readiness — a friendly conversation, not a test with a pass mark.

  3. 3

    Registration

    Your place is confirmed once the registration items are settled:

    Registration fee Security deposit Material fee First term fee Technology fee

    Questions on any of these? Email admission@victoriashire.edu.my.
